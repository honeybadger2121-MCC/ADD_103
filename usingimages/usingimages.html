How Simple Image Maps Work

The image map above uses basic rectangular coordinates that divide the image into four equal sections:

  • Top-left: coords="0,0,200,150" - starts at (0,0) and goes to (200,150)
  • Top-right: coords="200,0,400,150" - starts at (200,0) and goes to (400,150)
  • Bottom-left: coords="0,150,200,300" - starts at (0,150) and goes to (200,300)
  • Bottom-right: coords="200,150,400,300" - starts at (200,150) and goes to (400,300)

This simple approach eliminates coordinate confusion and makes the image map reliable!
