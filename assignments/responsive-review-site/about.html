About Badger Technologies

A fresh startup built on years of proven IT expertise. We combine real-world experience with entrepreneurial drive to deliver exceptional technology solutions for growing businesses.

Our Story

Founded: September 5, 2025

After years of working in IT and seeing businesses struggle with technology challenges, Benjamin Sherman founded Badger Technologies with a simple mission: provide reliable, expert IT support that every organization deserves.

With a foundation built on years of real-world experience in telecommunications at major industry leaders like Comcast and AT&T, combined with CompTIA certifications and specialized expertise in cybersecurity, network administration, and cloud systems management, Badger Technologies brings proven expertise to small and medium businesses.

We believe technology should empower your business, not frustrate it. Our mission is to be the trusted IT partner that businesses can rely on for comprehensive, proactive, and affordable technology solutions while they focus on growing their business.
