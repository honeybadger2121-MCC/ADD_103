About Badger Technologies

We're a dedicated team of IT professionals committed to securing, optimizing, and growing businesses across Illinois.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Badger Technologies emerged from a simple observation: small and medium-sized businesses in Illinois weren't getting the IT support they deserved. Too many companies struggled with outdated systems, security vulnerabilities, and IT teams who didn't understand their unique challenges.

We decided to change that. Starting with a dedicated team of five IT professionals, we've grown to help over 100+ businesses across Illinois transform their IT infrastructure, enhance security, and achieve their digital goals.
